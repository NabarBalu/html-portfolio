About Me

Hi there! I’m someone who firmly believes life is better when you’re laughing, exploring, or getting lost in a good story—preferably all three at once. I’ve got a pretty playful sense of humor, and I’m usually the one cracking jokes or finding the fun in everyday moments. Whether things are going perfectly or completely off the rails, I’ll probably be laughing about it either way.

Right now, I’m also working toward becoming a full stack developer—building my skills, learning new technologies, and slowly turning caffeine into code. It’s a journey I genuinely enjoy, especially when everything finally works… after hours of wondering why it didn’t. When I’m not coding or being hilarious (again, highly subjective), you’ll find me outdoors—traveling to new places, wandering through nature, or just soaking up fresh air. I love the feeling of discovering somewhere new, even if it’s just a hidden spot close to home.

I’m also a big fan of quieter escapes. Give me a good book and I’m gone for hours, or a game that pulls me into another world and I’m fully invested. It’s all about balance—adventure on one hand, cozy downtime on the other. If I had to sum it up: I like to keep life interesting, a little unpredictable, and always fun—while building something meaningful along the way.
